Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado toast is creamy, crisp and so satisfying. It's a delicious and simple breakfast, snack or light meal! It's best consumed immediately.

Ingredients

  • 1 slice sourdough bread, toasted
  • 1/2 cup mashed Haas avocado
  • 1 large hard boiled egg
  • 1 teaspoon Everything Bagel Seasoning

Steps

  1. Smear mashed avocado on toast. Sprinkle with everything bagel seasoning. Top with egg. Season with salt and pepper.
